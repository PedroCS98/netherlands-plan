How can I search for a job?

These are the core platforms in the Netherlands:

  • LinkedIn Jobs
  • Indeed Netherlands
  • I am Expat Jobs
  • Undutchables
  • Glassdoor Netherlands

For Valter, applying directy to companies is a good strategy, try Werk.nl (UWV official job portal), Randstad Technical (engineering + industry), Tempo-Team Technical, OTTO Work Force (industrial placements), VDL Groep Careers, DAF Trucks Careers and ASML Careers.

For Nuno, in complement to agencies these can be helpfull: ASML Jobs (huge target in Eindhoven), Philips Careers, VDL Groep, Randstad Technical Jobs, YoungCapital.

For Rafa agencies will be a hit, but you have the options of these as well: Tempo-Team Logistics, OTTO Work Force Logistics, Carrière Uitzendbureau, Albert Heijn Careers, Coolblue Jobs.

Lastly for Otis you have the extra resorces of Stack Overflow Jobs, Iamexpat Tech Jobs, AngelList and Wellfound
